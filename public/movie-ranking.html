The Best Books According to Ireland

My top 3 books of all-time.

Man's Search for Meaning by Viktor E. Frankl

This is the book that got me back into reading. I really think this is a book everyone should read at least once in their life.

Babel by R.F. Kuang

This is a brilliant book. I enjoyed every page. The story and the characters were so well thought out and incredibly engaging. I wish I could read this for the first time again.

Tomorrow, and Tomorrow, and Tomorrow by Gabrielle Zevin

It's very hard to describe this book. At its core, it is a book about two friends who make video games together, but the story is so much more. It has wonderful character development and does a beautiful job capturing what real life people would do.
